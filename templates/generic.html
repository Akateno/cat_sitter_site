All three feeders have a big button on the back, just click to open and close. The other ones are to register new cats, and from what it looks like graduate them?

We feed them morning, evening and right before we go to bed. We shoot for around:
Breakfast: 8:00am
(well usually wash their bowls and clean their mats in the morning so mold doesn't grow on them)
Dinner: 5:00pm
2nd Dinner: Before we go to bed
By no means do you have to feed them at these times but they will NOT leave you alone, and Kiwi will MEOW MEOW and MEOW until shes fed, and it gets VERY annoying.
