A guide to three horribly pampered cats

Found behind a gas station

Mango

Will freak out at the slighest sounds, does not enjoy being too close to Tony and will usually slap but never hurt tony. This is the one that loves pets and sleeping on Erick's basket. You can pick this one up if you want.

Kiwi

The definition of scaredy cat, she freaks out and hides when new people arrive. Shes fine after a couple of mins, (or hours) but is usually very chill. She is annoying when she knows its feeding time.

Kidnapped from the Seattle streets

Rigatoni (Tony)

Generic honestly, has the brain of an orange cat. Does NOT enjoy being picked up, but enjoys pets as long as hes on the floor. This one loves playing with the cat toy, and is the one that usually eats the most.

Just in case

Useful Tips

  • They might try to book it out the door so heads up
  • Even if you leave it open for 10 secs to grab something from the car, they WILL RUN
  • The girls dont really like Tony all that much so they try to steer clear from each others path
  • Tony sometimes tries to play with them but it ends up in a fight so we will usually play with tony to get rid of all that excess energy
  • The girls don't really care for toys
  • If they get in a spat well usually separate them and put one of them in the office for like 10 mins to cool off (90% of spats are between Tony and Mango)
